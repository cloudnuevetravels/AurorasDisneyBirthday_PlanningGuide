When to Go in January 2027

Crowd levels, events to know, and the best timing strategy for Aurora's birthday

⭐ Sweet Spot Recommendation
Based on historical patterns, January 9–14, 2027 is the projected best window — after New Year's break and Marathon Weekend crowds clear, and before the MLK Day holiday surge. Avoid January 14–19 (MLK Weekend is very busy) and the first week of January (Marathon Weekend + school breaks). Full January 2027 crowd guide →
🏃
RunDisney Marathon Weekend
Typically Jan 7–10. Thousands of runners + families fill the parks and All-Star resorts. Avoid this week for shorter waits. RunDisney Race Calendar ↗
🎨
EPCOT Festival of the Arts
Typically Jan 15–late Feb. Broadway shows, art & special food — included with your park ticket! A stunning bonus for an already magical trip. Festival info ↗
🌡️
January Weather
Average highs ~70°F (21°C), cool mornings in the 50s. No humidity. Pack layers for the little ones — mornings can be chilly but afternoons are perfect.
⏰
Shorter Park Hours
January parks often close 8–9pm vs. 11pm in peak season. Plan fireworks and character dining timing accordingly. Bonus: shorter lines throughout the day!
🎡
Skyliner Refurbishment
Disney historically closes the Skyliner gondola for maintenance in late January (~Jan 25–31). Pop Century & Art of Animation guests use buses during this window.

Transportation — Car-Free Guide

Rudsena does not drive. Here is every transportation option needed to get from the airport to the parks and back.

✅ Good News
A Disney World trip is 100% manageable without a car — especially in January when crowds are lighter and wait times for buses are shorter. Every hotel on this list provides complimentary transportation to all parks.
✈️
MCO Airport → Resort
Mears Connect is Disney's preferred shared shuttle from Orlando International Airport — reliable, affordable, direct to Disney hotels. Book in advance. Mears Connect ↗ · Uber/Lyft also available (~$30–$45 from MCO).
🚌
Disney Buses (On-Property)
All 4 on-property resorts include free Disney bus service to all parks, Disney Springs & water parks. Buses run ~1 hour before park open to ~1 hour after close. No reservations needed. Disney Transportation Guide ↗
🚡
Disney Skyliner (Gondola)
Pop Century & Art of Animation guests get gondola access to EPCOT & Hollywood Studios. Aurora will love floating through the sky! Note: late January refurb ~Jan 25–31 means buses only during that window. Skyliner info ↗
⛵
Boats & Monorail
Port Orleans guests can take a boat to Disney Springs. Disney monorail connects Magic Kingdom area resorts to the park and Ticket & Transportation Center. Not available from Value resorts.
🏨
Drury Hotel Shuttle
Free hourly Drury shuttle to all 4 parks, Disney Springs & water parks. No reservation needed for standard service. Late-night return service on select event nights. Drury transportation info ↗
📍
Disney Springs Bus Backup
If staying at Drury, the 15-min walk to Disney Springs gives access to free Disney buses to all parks — a great backup on busy shuttle days. Disney Springs is also worth visiting on its own for shopping & dining!
